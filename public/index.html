The Isle Evrima Dinosaur Speed Chart

Frequently Asked Questions

Which is the fastest dinosaur in The Isle Evrima?

The fastest herbivore in The Isle Evrima is the Gallimimus (Galli), reaching a maximum speed of 56.9 km/h.

The fastest carnivore is the Carnotaurus (Carno), with a top speed of 55.6 km/h.

What is the maximum speed of a Prime Allosaurus?

A fully grown Prime Allosaurus reaches a maximum running speed of 39.4 km/h.

When the Allosaurus becomes 100 growth, its maximum speed is reduced to 35.8 km/h.
